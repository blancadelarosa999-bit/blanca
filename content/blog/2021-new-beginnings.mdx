To say that 2020 has been a difficult year would be an understatement. 2020 pushed many of us to isolation, despair and grief.

Yet in every ending, there is a beginning. As we look toward the new year, we carry with us the lessons learned — about resilience, about community, about what truly matters.

New beginnings don't require a clean slate. They require courage, hope, and the willingness to take that first step forward, even when the path ahead is uncertain.
