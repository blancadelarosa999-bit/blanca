Nonverbal communication represents between 60-90% of your daily responses and occurs continuously during interactions with others. Your body language is a physical manifestation of your internal thoughts.

When individuals harbor feelings of inadequacy and worthlessness, they tend to manifest circumstances and relationships that reinforce these negative self-perceptions. When someone feels unworthy, their nonverbal cues communicate this vulnerability to others, who may subsequently treat that person with disrespect and take advantage of them.

Understanding and mastering your body language is a critical skill for professional success. The way you carry yourself, make eye contact, and use gestures speaks volumes before you ever say a word. Learn to project confidence through your physical presence — it is one of the most powerful tools in your career arsenal.
