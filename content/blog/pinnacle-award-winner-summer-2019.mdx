We are thrilled to announce that *Pursuing a Better Tomorrow* has been chosen as one of the **NABE Pinnacle Book Achievement Award Winners** in the Memoir Category for Summer 2019.

The Pinnacle Book Achievement Awards are presented by the North American Book Awards and recognize excellence in independent publishing. This recognition is a tremendous honor and a testament to the power of immigrant stories and the pursuit of a better tomorrow.

Thank you to everyone who has supported this journey.
