Drawing from 41 years of professional experience, *A Holistic Approach to Your Career* offers guidance on achieving career success through a balanced, whole-person approach.

Key topics include navigating workplace dynamics, managing difficult supervisors, recovering from career setbacks, creating effective career roadmaps, and developing self-empowerment strategies. The author emphasizes combining practical advice with inner wisdom.

Whether you're a recent graduate, a new employee, or a career professional seeking advancement, this book provides the tools and perspectives you need to take control of your professional journey.
