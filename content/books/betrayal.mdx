Camila and Nic's marriage begins to crumble after their relocation from New York to the Virginia suburbs. As Camila struggles to adapt to suburban life and the pressures of maternal expectations, and Nic becomes consumed by work, they grow apart.

When Camila's extramarital affair is discovered, it devastates Nic and destabilizes their entire family. *The Betrayal* traces both characters on their separate journeys — Camila toward self-discovery and redemption, Nic toward healing and forgiveness — while both navigate the need to provide stability for their children.

A raw, honest exploration of what happens when trust is broken and whether love can survive the aftermath.
