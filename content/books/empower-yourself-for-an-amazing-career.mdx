Published by Balboa Press, a Division of Hay House, *Empower Yourself for an Amazing Career* draws from 34 years of corporate experience to address the challenges that can derail even the most promising careers.

This book covers how to assess the journey of your career, how to manage a bad manager, how to deal with career failure, and how to develop a career road map. Through personal anecdotes and practical strategies, Blanca shares both her successes and her mistakes with refreshing transparency.
