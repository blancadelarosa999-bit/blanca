*What would you give up today for a better tomorrow?*

From humble beginnings, despite myriad challenges, a life of untold blessings and opportunities unfolds. *Pursuing a Better Tomorrow* is a multi-generational memoir spanning over a century, following characters from Spain to the United States.

The narrative weaves together four interconnected stories examining immigration, acculturation, and self-discovery across different eras. It incorporates the rich historical context of the Taíno people, the conquistadors, and the Dominican Republic, while tracing Blanca's own journey from New York City's projects to corporate America.

This is a story about the sacrifices we make, the dreams that drive us, and the resilience that carries us from one generation to the next.
