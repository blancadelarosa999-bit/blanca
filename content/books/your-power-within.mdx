Written following the passing of her husband after 51 years of marriage, *Your Power Within — Inner Guidance* explores the soul's journey and the quest for purpose and inner strength.

This deeply personal work emphasizes patience and gradual progress in discovering your untapped potential. It examines spiritual development and personal transformation across every domain of life — health, career, relationships, and emotional well-being.

Within each of us lies an untapped well of strength, waiting to be discovered. This book is your guide to finding it.
