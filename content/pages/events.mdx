## Speaking and workshop topics

Blanca speaks and teaches from a foundation of lived experience: immigration, corporate leadership, mentorship, grief, faith, and the pursuit of personal reinvention.

### Career empowerment

Discover practical strategies for getting your career back on track, clarifying your next step, and building the confidence required to move forward intentionally.

### Personal brand and communication

Learn how to define your personal brand, communicate your value, and present yourself with clarity in resumes, interviews, and professional settings.

### Author conversations and readings

Invite Blanca to speak about her books, her immigrant story, and the themes of resilience, healing, reinvention, and hope that run through her catalogue.

### Inner guidance and spiritual growth

Blanca also offers reflective conversations on grief, purpose, patience, and spiritual development for audiences seeking encouragement and deeper perspective.
